DESCRIPTION

Our CBD Kinesiology tape is latex free and uses a
sweat and waterproof adhesive. Each roll
contains an organic blend of CBD rich hemp
distillate and other natural ingredients designed
to support your muscles and joints for effective
pain relief and healing to the injured area.

With correct strapping, the kinesiology tape is
designed to support your joints, assist in
lymphatic drainage, increase localized
circulation and activate the endogenous
analgesic system, all whilst maintaining full
range of motion.

 RECOMMENDED DOSE

For best results please apply to appropriate area
before activity. Clean the skin, remove the paper
packing from the tape and apply to area. Try not
to overstretch the tape. When removing, do not
rip off as adhesive can be strong.
