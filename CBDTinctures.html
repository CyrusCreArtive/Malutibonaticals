DESCRIPTION

Our CBD tincture is expertly crafted to create a
natural, reliable, and easy to use daily tincture.

 RECOMMENDED DOSE

While there are many ways to use a tincture, we
recommend placing a dropper onto the mouth
and hold it for 30 seconds or longer before
swallowing. Tinctures can also be added to foods
and drinks.

• Quality: This tincture is created in a GMP

• Warning: Please adhere to the recommended
